Steam Bug

The Steam Bug is a simple machine that uses water for cleaning of different items. The major advantage is that this machine eliminates the stress of hard scrubbing on items such as furniture / fitting thereby prolonging the lifespan. It is electrically operated and can be used in any part of a building or facility.

It uses only water converted into hot water for steam cleaning and can be used to clean virtually such as all toilets fittings such as WC, WHB, Urinary bowls etc. It can be used for total restoration cleaning of all fitting which is convenient and saves time.

Host Dry Extraction Machine

This is the last rug-clean method, which uses power (sponges) without water to wash the rug leaving it clean and dry. It has a vacuum system which removes dirt in rug. It is done by way of penetrating through the fibers of the rug.

It is faster, easy, convenient, and ideal for deep cleaning and stain resistant carpet

Knap Sac

Used for spraying (fumigation) indoor and outdoor. This has an edge over motorized fumigation machine as it reaches corners that the machine cannot.

Fog

It is also known as smoke fogging machine. When functional it mixes chemical into smoke form which is used indoor and outdoor to reach areas that the chemical applied with knap sac cannot get to. By this, the insects are killed.

Cimex Machine

This works like the slow speed scrubber. It makes use of three brushes and is used for scrubbing of very dirty rugs. It has the ability of scrubbing off dirt inside the fiber of the rug.

Leather (Upholstery) Washing Machine

This is used in washing leather materials whether in car, home or offices. It is handy and easy to use.

Speed Scrubbing Machine

It is used for washing and stripping of all hard floors such as ceramics, terrazzo marble, granite etc and buffing. It also does the job of the buffer and scrubber.

It works more effectively in terms of speed than the ordinary one which is also referred to as slow speed scrubbing machine.

Double engine vacuum machine

This is a two in one vacuum cleaner with very high suction power. It is used for the extraction of soils, water and dust from all areas where hand cannot reach. It is very suitable for use in cleaning the ATM.

Single engine vacuum machine

Aqua 70: for extraction of dust and soils (liquid) from rugs to hard Floor mostly used for heavy soils.

Aqua 40: for extraction of dust and soils (liquid) from floors and rugs for not too complicated jobs.

Dry Type: for extraction of dust from rugs, blinds, tracks curtains etc.

Steam Bug: for steam washing of toilets.

Fumigation and pest control machines

Knap Sac: This is the manual fumigation machine that is specifically used in areas or cases of direct impact.

Motorize jet Sprayer: This is a more advanced method of fumigation. In this case, due to the mechanized engine of the machine, the pressure of spray is far higher than that of the Knap sack.

Fogging Machines: Otherwise known as the swing fog; this machine converts chemicals from liquid to gaseous form and the smoke produced reaches crevices where unwanted pests resides.

Host machine

It is used for dry cleaning of rugs and extraction of dust without the use of water. This machine can only be used with chemical in powder form.

Blower/Dryer

It is used for drying rugs.

Ultrasonic cleaning machine

Cleaning ultrasonically comes with several advantages:

  • Crevices and small holes can be reached where conventional methods may not.
  • Oils and inorganic particles can be removed.
  • Reduce solvent expenses
  • Reduction in health hazards.
  • Cleans all types of electronics like copiers, TV sets, computers etc.
  • Effective fire restoration cleaning.
  • Cleaning of window blinds.

The Trojan floor restoration machine

The Trojan floor restoration machine is otherwise known as the floor honing machine. This machine is an extremely low speed machine that is been used in restoration of floors like terrazzo, marble and granite with the use of pellets called the diamond disc.
